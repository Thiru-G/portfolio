Description

An advanced AIoT (Artificial Intelligence of Things) fleet tracking system developed for Dubai Airport, achieving 35% higher accuracy than traditional GPS-based solutions. The system utilizes beacon mesh networking technology to provide real-time location tracking and management of airport fleet vehicles.

The solution integrates advanced AIOT concepts including office management systems and precise fleet tracking capabilities. The beacon mesh network ensures reliable indoor and outdoor tracking, making it ideal for complex airport environments.
