Drug Susceptibility Testing Equipment

close

Description

Advanced medical equipment for drug susceptibility testing, developed as part of a government-funded initiative. This project was supported by NIDHI-PRAYAS and NIDHI-SSS grants in collaboration with NIRT (ICMR), representing a significant contribution to medical research infrastructure.

The equipment enables precise and automated testing of drug susceptibility, streamlining laboratory workflows and improving accuracy in medical diagnostics. The system integrates hardware prototyping, sensor technology, and automation to deliver reliable results.
