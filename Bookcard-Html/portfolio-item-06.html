close

Description

Advanced IoT-based telemedicine equipment designed for Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) diagnosis, developed in collaboration with Apollo Hospitals and VIT Chennai. This innovative system enables remote patient monitoring and diagnosis, significantly reducing the time required for diagnostic processes.

The equipment integrates IoT sensors, edge computing capabilities, and telemedicine protocols to provide real-time health monitoring. It enables healthcare professionals to remotely assess patient conditions and make informed diagnostic decisions.

Apollo Hospitals Pvt. Ltd. & VIT Chennai
